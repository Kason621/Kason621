About Me

I am a high school student with a strong passion for both creative expression and analytical thinking. My primary interest lies in photography, where I have explored diverse cultures and landscapes across countries such as China, Singapore, Japan, and France. Through my lens, I aim to capture authentic stories and meaningful moments, often working independently to fully immerse myself in the creative process.


Beyond photography, I have a deep interest in economics and mathematics. I enjoy tackling challenging problems, participating in academic discussions, and continuously pushing my intellectual limits. My dedication has led me to compete in contests such as AMC and BMO, where I was honored with a Gold Award.


I am also actively engaged in community service. Volunteering at a local library and traveling to rural Yunnan to photograph children have allowed me to connect with different communities and use photography as a storytelling tool. Currently, I am planning a photography exhibition to share my work and the stories behind each image.


Outside academics, I enjoy playing basketball as part of my school team. I am also a fan of K-pop, especially Babymonster, and greatly admire Ahyeon. Additionally, I have a unique interest in collecting Crocin products.
